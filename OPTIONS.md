## Notes
- Step 1 options focus on parsing, OCR, layout, boxes, tables, and document structure extraction.
- Step 2 options focus on de-identification after parsing or directly on rendered page images.
- Licensing may differ between repository code and downloadable model weights; verify exact terms before production use.
- For this workspace, Marker, PaddleOCR PP-StructureV3, and MinerU are direct local CPU paths, while OLMOCR is routed to the GB10 machine over SSH with Docker.
